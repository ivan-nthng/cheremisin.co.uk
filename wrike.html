Implement it into a working service

Evaluating the interface

  • Ability to atart and finish a sprint
  • Entry level
  • Time spent on organizing the process

Problem

Today, most product teams work on agile planning frameworks. If you are developing project management software, the first thing the customer will demand from you is the right tool to do their job well, otherwise they will go to your competitor to get it.

We didn't need to invent the frameworks themselves, we needed to understand how to implement the ability to start and finish sprints with the current technical features of Wrike, how to organize the backlog and how digging in legacy algorithms not to screw something up in the core product.

Realization

Unfortunately I have nothing to show, the whole project is under strict NDA. We finished the MVP and the coronavirus began. The project was put on hold for a while and I had to leave. But this was a team of professionals. Engineers and designers of the highest level, I learned a lot while working with them.
