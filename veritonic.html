Research Case

Two groups listen to the company's audio assets. One group has heard these assets before, while the other hears them for the first time. After listening, both groups answer the client's questions. Based on their responses, a report is generated for the client. Initially, CS managers handled the entire process. They surveyed clients via email, filled out questionnaires, sent data to panellists providers, compiled presentations, and presented to clients. These are my primary sources of information.

Problem

High entry barrier and inability for third parties to use research capabilities.

Only a CS manager can create a study by receiving all necessary information from the client via email or Word files. This makes it impossible to quickly collaborate with third parties or within a team, and to independently use research capabilities

User Goal

Evaluate the effectiveness of their audio advertising or compare their assets with competitors.

Basic Scenario

Gather all information about the target audience, based on communication goals

Create a questionnaire for the target user group

Survey panelists and collect the data.

Analyze the data.

Present the results to the client.

Interface Requirements

User completes all scenario steps within a single interface.
