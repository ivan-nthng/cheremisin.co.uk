Peruse

An algorithm that helps analyze large volumes of documents and sort them by linking them together depending on their relationships.

Problem

In a trucking company, most of the paperwork is done manually. Finding the correct Bill of Lading (BOL) for Rate Confirmation can take a lot of time, and the potential for human error is also a concern. The efficiency of this process directly affects how quickly the truck driver receives payment.

One significant problem in this business arises from the characteristics of the people involved. Even if a new software is interesting, there is no time to understand it. As a result, people postpone the implementation and then forget about it.

The Peruse ML algorithm can perform this task in 20 seconds, regardless of the amount of data.

Tasks

Create a simple demo tool that shows how Peruse can save time for its customers.

Research

I interviewed people who do this work, and the main problems were evident. Many users work with outdated technology. They may not be young and their level of technology proficiency may be superficial. Therefore, the entry threshold should be minimal.

The workflow consists of two steps: working with an array of documents and working with data contained within the documents.

Hypothesis

The service should work like magic for the user. With just one movement of the hand, a large amount of data can be transformed into structured information. This solves the problems of all user groups. However, everything must be done as quickly as possible.

Solution

I transferred the analog workflow to an electronic medium. The user now works with the document array and the data in the documents separately.

Introduction

A demo is necessary to showcase the strength of the algorithm and to collect personal data. The process of getting to know each other should be quick and painless. We only request basic information. Immediately after that, we ask for any type of document, even a photo of cats or family, as we will find and sort out the necessary pairs.

Working with documents

After uploading, we analyze each document and match it to its corresponding document(s), which can be several. We also specify the probability with which these documents match each other.

After completing the analysis, the user has the option to download and send all the documents to the accounting department or other relevant parties. Alternatively, they can work with the data and understand how it all fits together. This leads to the second user scenario, which involves working with the data in the documents.

Working with data

Simply finding matching numbers is not sufficient to search for paired documents; it is also important to understand the context. While a robot couldn't do this before, Peruse is a useful tool because it understands context. All recognized data can be viewed, downloaded, or edited if the algorithm did not recognize something or recognized it incorrectly.

Edit documents

Drivers often send documents that are photographed in the dark or of poor quality. In such cases, we edit them on our side. However, if some data is not recognized, our service provides a simple editor for further editing.
