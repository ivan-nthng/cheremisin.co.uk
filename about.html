Hi, I have been working as a product designer for nine years. It became my profession from a hobby that I loved very much. Interfaces are all around us, and over the years, I have learned that the best interface is no interface.

About me

I was born in Leningrad, USSR, and am now a 33-year-old artist based Brazil. I love film photography, surfing, blues music, and coding. My parents and I traveled extensively throughout Europe, which allowed me to develop an open-minded appreciation for the beauty and functionality of everything around me from an early age.

Half of my family is in Ukraine, and as I write this, their homes are being bombed by the Russian army. I do not support the actions of the Russian government and am actively doing everything in my power to help Ukrainians.

Art

Nothing | Nichego

"Nothing" or "Nichego" in Russian, is considered the national idea of Russia. If you ask any Russian what they want, they will respond with "leave me alone". Throughout their existence, Russians have focused on survival rather than living.

Nothing happens without a reason. One of my goals is to draw Russians' attention to the fact that life goes on, and there are many interesting things beyond their daily routines.

Photography

Documentary filming helps me pay attention to the world around me. It allows me to see familiar things in a new light and explore new things.
