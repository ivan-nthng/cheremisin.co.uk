Ivan Cheremisin

Born in Leningrad, USSR, I'm now a 33-year-old Brazil-based artist with passions for film photography, surfing, blues, and coding. Extensive European travel in my youth fostered an appreciation for diverse beauty and functionality.

I have family in Ukraine who have been affected by the Russian invasion. I strongly oppose the Russian government's actions and actively support the Ukrainian people.

Art

Nothing | Nichego

"Nichego" (meaning "nothing") is often seen as Russia's national idea. Many Russians, when asked what they want, simply say "leave me alone." Historically, Russians have prioritized survival over living fully. My goal is to show Russians that life offers more than just daily routines, encouraging them to explore beyond their usual experiences.

Photography

Documentary filming helps me pay attention to the world around me. It allows me to see familiar things in a new light and explore new things.

Fixed Gear

As my daily mode of transportation, I have always preferred bicycles. For the past 8 years, I have been riding a fixed gear bike as well.
